You’re Never Too Old to Learn Like a Kid

Course Leaders: Sheilagh Johnson and Tom Naps

Welcome to the course home page for the fall term OLLI course “You’re Never Too Old to Learn Like a Kid ”. The course will meet in the Coffey Computer Lab (Room 50) for three Fridays 12:30 - 2:00 starting November 1. Feel free to contact Tom (mailto:thomas.naps@gmail.com) anytime you have questions or concerns about the course.

Course description from OLLI catalog: These days, kids are actively learning across all disciplines by using technology. While it may be true that some children spend too much time on social media, technology also has tremendous potential for helping us to help them learn better. We’ll explore that upside in hands-on fashion, providing a curated review of online educational resources for a variety of subjects and letting you playfully explore them. And who knows? Along the way we might learn a lot about ourselves and our own ability to better communicate with the technology-obsessed youngsters in our lives.

Course outline
